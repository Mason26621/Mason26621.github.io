About me

My name is Mason Wells. I enjoy video games of any genre, music of (almost) any genre, and sleeping. I mostly chose to learn programming so I could make my own games and to make mods for games that I like. Some of my current favorite games are Touhou 18: Unconnected Marketeers, Disgaea 2, and Garry's Mod. My current favorite song is Fly Me To The Moon sung by Claire. Recently I bought a computer so I can finally properly enjoy these games, especially Disgaea 2 due to a bug where it is incompattible with some graphics cards. I have two dogs, a golden retriever named Luna and a miniature schnauzer named Ava. I aspire to become a game developer for Bethesda in the future, and to make my own personal indie games.
